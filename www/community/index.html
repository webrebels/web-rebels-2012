We love you!

Giving something back to our community

Web Rebels 2012 was a blast! We are still buzzing with excitement after having hosted our first ever conference. It was an amazing two days and we couldn’t have done this without the support of some of the web developer community in Norway. Our community is as viral as ever and a lot of brilliant people are putting in a lot of work. We know that pulling the strings in a community can be a lot of work and without financial support you can not accomplish everything you want to.

The Web Rebels have decided to use 30% of this years profits from ticket sales to help the web developer community. If you host a meet up or similar community event you can send us an email and we might help you out.

What we cover and how to apply

Here is what we cover. If what you think of is not on the list, we're not covering it.

  • Costs for a speakers travel to and from the event
  • Accommodation for the speaker
  • Fee for having a meet up registered at meetup.com

Applying for support is pretty easy, just shoot us an email at community@webrebels.org and we will get back to you. If you want to make things happen quickly, here some stuff you should include: Your name, link to previous events, an outline of what you want to do and what you need us to support.

Our list of demands

There is _always_ a catch

There is no free lunch and if you do not meet our demands, we can not support you.

  • Your event is open to everyone and free
  • Your event is not tied to any commercial activity
  • Your event needs to be about web development related topics

Who is this for?

Unfortunatly not everyone can apply..

Currently we can only support meet up's or community events in Scandinavia. This might change, but currently that is just how it is for now. That does not mean we love the communities in the rest of the world any less.

What is this?

The purpose of this guide.

Oslo has consistently been ranked as one of the most expensive cities on the planet. Unfortunately this makes it hard for people coming here as everything is really expensive. However, there are actually ways of visiting Oslo without getting financially ruined. This is what we wanted all of you to know, because we do care about every one of our attendants.

Review
Directions

Hai is a family run cafe which has been serving hungry rebels in Oslo amazing Vietnamese food for years. Not only is their food realy nice, but also the prices are really nice compared to the rest of the city.

Illegal Burger

Cousine: Hamburgers

Directions.

One of our favourite burger joints with excellent food at a very decent price. This is fast food at it's best, you'll be in and out in ten minutes.

Hells Kitchen Oslo

Reviews

Homepage

Directions

One of the worlds best baristas, Tim Wendelboe, has set up shop at Grünerløkka in Oslo. This is an amazing place where they burn their own coffe and you can watch them perform their magic while enjoying a good espresso.

Stockfelths

Homepage

Directions
